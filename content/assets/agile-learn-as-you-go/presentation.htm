Ball point game

Play as a team

  • Count the number of balls that did a full-round (everyone touched the ball)
  • You cannot pass the ball to your left-hand or right-hand neighbour
  • A ball that touched the floor cannot be counted anymore
  • Play in iterations, 2 min planning, 1 min play

The agile process

Plan, do, check, act

  1. Have an overarching objective to foster self-organization
  2. Break down your objective into key results to be achieved within 2 or 3 months
  3. Deliver towards the key results with limited work in progress
  4. Check while you work and adapt

Self-organization is a force of life

We want to make use of self-organization by giving it a direction, and not micro-manage it.

Scaling agile

means you need more than one team to deliver a thing

Conway´s law

Your system architecture will mirror your organization's communication structure.

Conway´s law in practice

Small and distributes teams lead to a modular service architecture.

Large co-located teams lead to a monolithic architecture.

Seek for loosely coupled teams

Agile will scale itself

You do not need a scaling framework.

Teams will spread word about useful practices and listening teams will adapt.

Try to keep this

Agile means completing meaningful things in short intervals, to foster learning, while collaborating as a team.
